TIPS

Diet Searching

Through a good look at somebodies diet, an understanding should be immediately taken place through the excess amount of unhealthy foods your average person has per day. This could also act as a wake-up call to the person by simply looking at the overview of their diet.

Cutting Back

Cutting back is a major play in the tips I give people as unhealthy foods is one of the main things people struggle with within their everyday diet.

Recommendations

Recommendations will mostly be cutting back, such as fast food, high sugar items, and balancing out your diet with proper proteins, vitamins, fats & carbohydrates.

Results

Results just include the differences in feeling, weight, and overall healthiness once the new diet/help given has been implemented for a couple of weeks.

Possibilities

Possibilities as in the different things that may be caused by the food you are currently eating within your diet. Being told the different health risks at play/possible it helps people understand that their diet needs a serious change.
